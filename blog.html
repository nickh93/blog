Survey Dinosaur

Survey Dinosaur is an designed to match users with compatible surveys that compensate the user. Traditional survey platforms fail to correctly match users with surveys making it incovenienet and time-consuming for the user. Survey Dinasour stores user information via recognition and then uses that matches users to applicable surveys.
